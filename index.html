INTERNSHIP ONBOARDING PROCESS

Step-by-Step Guide for New Interns  | SNTI, Tata Steel, Jamshedpur,Jharkhand

#WeAlsoMakeTomorrow
FOLLOW ALL STEPS CAREFULLY TO BEGIN YOUR INTERNSHIP AT TATA STEEL PLANT
01
Fill the Tata Parikshan Form
Register on the Tata Steel Internship Portal and complete the Tata Parikshan application form with all required academic and personal details.
02
Wait for Official Acceptance Email
After submitting the form, wait for the official acceptance / confirmation email from Tata Steel. Do NOT proceed to the next step before receiving this email.
03
Contact Concerned Persons for Gate Pass Details
Once the acceptance email is received, contact for gate pass process guidance:
 • Abhiram Jha Sir — 9263632892     • Sumit Sir • Diksha Chopra — 9113119093   
04
Visit SNTI 'N' Road Office, Bistupur, Jamshedpur
Visit the SNTI office at N Road, Bistupur, Jamshedpur for Gate Pass processing. If you are carrying a laptop, get your Laptop Pass processed here as well.
05
Wait for Gate Pass Ready Email (1–2 Working Days)
Processing takes 1–2 working days. Wait for the official email from the SNTI Gate Pass Department confirming your gate pass is ready — only then go and collect your physical gate pass.
06
Arrange PPE Kit & Contact for Any Doubts
Contact: Abhiram Jha Sir (9263632892) & Sumit Sir () & Diksha Chopra (9113119093)for PPE / training doubts.
PPE Kit (Available at Sakchi & Bistupur Market) :  1. Safety Jacket (Orange)    2. Safety Shoes(Black)    3. Safety Helmet (Yellow/Blue)
⚠ PPE
MANDATORY
07
Enter Tata Steel Plant with Gate Pass + RFID Card
Wear your complete PPE Kit and carry your Gate Pass + RFID Card mandatorily. Entry without complete PPE will be strictly DENIED at the gate.
08
Call Your Guide — Begin Your Internship! 🎓
Call your assigned guide, confirm their location inside the plant, report to them and officially start your internship project.
⚠️
IMPORTANT NOTE — PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT (PPE)
For Vocational Training, every student must wear the complete PPE Kit — entry inside the Tata Steel Plant will be denied without it.
PPE Kit:  1. Safety Jacket (Orange)  |  2. Safety Shoes(Black)  |  3. Safety Helmet (Yellow or Blue)
Easily available at: Sakchi Market and Bistupur Market, Jamshedpur.
